Bring your Hikvision AX PRO wireless alarm system into Homey. The app talks directly to the alarm panel over your local network, with no cloud account and no separate bridge, and turns your panel, areas, detectors and peripherals into Homey devices you can watch and automate.

Arm and disarm by area, follow each detector's motion, door, smoke, temperature, battery and tamper state, get a live snapshot when a camera detector triggers, and build Flows around any of it. Everything runs locally on your Homey and your panel credentials never leave it.
